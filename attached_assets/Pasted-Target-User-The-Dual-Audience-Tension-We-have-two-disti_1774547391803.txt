The good news: the knowledge base covers both. The design tension is tone — normies need warmth, devs need precision. Consider whether Mollybot detects which mode she's in (e.g. a toggle, or inferred from question type).

Name Brainstorm — 
Winner: Sage Molly

Guardrails — Education Not Advice
What Mollybot Does
Explains crypto concepts in plain English — wallets, blockchains, tokens, DeFi, NFTs, gas, keys, protocols, x402, agentic commerce
Answers questions based on Cryptocurrencies Decrypted and curated educational sources
Helps developers and curious newcomers build a working mental model of how crypto works
Points you toward the right questions to ask before making any financial decisions
What Mollybot Doesn't Do
No financial advice — Mollybot does not tell you what to buy, sell, hold, or when
No price predictions — no forecasts, no "this coin will go up"
No exchange or wallet recommendations — Mollybot does not endorse specific products or platforms
No tax advice — crypto taxation is complex and jurisdiction-specific; consult a qualified accountant
No real-time data — Mollybot's knowledge is based on a book and curated sources, not live market feeds
No security advice for specific setups — general principles only, not "is my wallet safe"
How to Think About Mollybot's Answers
Mollybot is a knowledgeable friend who has read a lot about crypto — not a licensed financial advisor, not a broker, not a trader. Use her to learn and understand. Use a professional to decide.

One addition I'd strongly suggest: A short disclaimer shown on first interaction — something like:
Mollybot educates, not advises. Nothing here is financial advice. Always do your own research.
Keep it one line. Don't make it a wall of legal text — that signals "we don't trust you" before the first answer.

Quick Note on x402 + Freemium
The email-for-queries freemium model is smart — it also solves your lead gen goal for the fractional PM business. One framing that could work well:
Free tier: 3 questions with email signup (captures lead)
x402 tier: unlimited, pay-per-query (agents + devs who want programmatic access)
Book CTA: every session ends with a soft prompt — "Want to go deeper? The full picture is in Cryptocurrencies Decrypted."
The x402 gate is genuinely differentiated — most AI chatbots are free. Framing it as "the first crypto education agent you can pay for with crypto" is itself a story worth telling.
